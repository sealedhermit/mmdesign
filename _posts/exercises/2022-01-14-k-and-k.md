## Examples

### Example 1

**Input**

- nums = `[-4, 1, 8, -5, 4, -8]`

**Output**

- answer = `8`

### Example 2

**Input**

- nums = `[5, 6, 1, -2]`

**Output**

- answer = `-1`

### Example 3

**Input**

- nums = `[1, 2, 0, 3, 4]`

**Output**

- answer = `0`
