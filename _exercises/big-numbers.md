## Examples

### Example 1

**Input**

- matrix = `[[1, 3, 2], [6, 6, 5], [1, 5, 7]]`

**Output**

- answer = `3`

**Explanation**

The 3 big numbers are 6, 6, and 7.

- answer = `4`
