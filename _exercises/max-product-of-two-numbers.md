## Examples

### Example 1

**Input**

- nums = `[5, 1, 7]`

**Output**

- answer = `35`

**Explanation**

35 is the largest product that can be made from 5 \* 7

### Example 2

**Input**

- nums = `[7, 1, 7]`

**Output**

- answer = `49`

**Explanation**

49 is the largest product that can be made from 7 \* 7. The values can be the same but they must be separate elements.

### Example 3

**Input**

- nums = `[-5, 1, -7]`

**Output**

- answer = `35`

**Explanation**

35 is the largest product that can be made from -5 \* -7.
