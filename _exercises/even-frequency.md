## Examples

### Example 1

**Input**

- nums = `[2, 4, 4, 2, 3, 3]`

**Output**

- answer = `True`

**Explanation**

Every number occurs twice.

### Example 2

**Input**

- nums = `[1]`

**Output**

- answer = `False`

**Explanation**

`1` occurs an odd number of times.
